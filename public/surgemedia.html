Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of brands can advertise on your clinic screens?
Any brand targeting the general Malaysian population. Our audience is everyday Malaysians visiting GP clinics — not a niche health audience. We've placed campaigns for health supplements, insurance, lifestyle brands, and local businesses. All content is reviewed against Malaysian advertising guidelines before going live.
How do you measure campaign performance?
We use QR codes on your creative to track anonymous, aggregate engagement — scan rates by clinic location and time window. Monthly performance reports cover estimated reach and QR scan data. No individual patient data is collected or stored.
Do you also offer digital marketing services?
Yes. Alongside in-clinic advertising, Surge Media offers social media management, content creation, Google review management, and branding — primarily for clinics and health brands wanting a complete marketing presence.
What are the minimum contract terms?
Our starter package runs on a 3-month minimum. The Impact package is 6 months. Tailored packages are typically 12-month partnerships. We're a startup and believe in earning your business — we'll always be transparent about what you're committing to.
Where is the clinic network located?
We're currently live in Selangor and growing. If your campaign requires specific zones or clinic types, get in touch and we'll tell you what's available and what's coming.
